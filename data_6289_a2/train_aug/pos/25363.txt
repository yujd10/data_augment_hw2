Doraemon uses the magic tools of the future to work with Nobita to build his own land of clouds. The floating land of clouds stops in the Alps. Nobita saves a mysterious boy, who soon disappears. Suddenly the real land of the cloud appeared before them. But they were placed under the supervision of the heavenly people and could not return to the ground. It was further learned that the heavenly people were plotting a Noah plan to wipe out the evil human beings of the earth with floods.
